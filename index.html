Introduction

Hi, I’m Obi Batbileg. I grew up on the vast steppe, where endless skies meet open land, and where the design language of Soviet-era architecture and industrial structures shaped much of the visual world around me. I first explored photography through digital formats, but over the past two years I shifted to film.

My Photography Story

I began taking photographs in middle school. From the start, sharing my perspective through images was a joyful hobby — one that gradually became my creative outlet.

Each frame is both a discovery and a record, a frozen fragment of time. It is both memory and presence, an artifact that allows us to return, to sense, and to feel, exactly as it once was.
